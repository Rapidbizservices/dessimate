Industrial Motor Controller

  • Ideal for conveyor systems, pumps, or robotic arms

Home Appliance Controller

  • Charge and monitor NiMH or Li-Ion battery packs
  • Use ADCs to monitor voltage/current/temperature
  • PWM control for current-limited charging
