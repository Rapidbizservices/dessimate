🌿 Sustainability as a Service

Partnering for a Greener Supply Chain

At Dessimate, sustainability is not just part of how we operate — it's a service we deliver. We help larger companies strengthen their environmental and social responsibility programs by offering sustainable manufacturing, sourcing, and reporting solutions at every stage of the supply chain.

By integrating responsible practices into your mechanical parts sourcing, you can meet your ESG targets, enhance your brand reputation, and contribute meaningfully to a cleaner, greener future.

What We Offer

♻️ Sustainable Manufacturing Solutions

ISO-certified foundries using energy-efficient processes, closed-loop scrap recycling, and lean manufacturing to minimize waste at every step.

🔎 Sustainable Sourcing and Traceability

Full material traceability from raw material to finished part, with vetted suppliers and optimized logistics to reduce your supply chain's carbon footprint.

📊 Reporting and Compliance Support

Carbon footprint data, supplier certifications, and alignment with GRI, CDP, and SEC climate disclosure frameworks to meet your reporting obligations.

🔧 Eco-Design and Engineering

Lightweighting, alloy selection for recyclability, and DFM consulting to design greener components that support your product innovation roadmaps.

Why Choose Dessimate?

  • Expand Your ESG Program: Align sourcing with global sustainability benchmarks
  • Strengthen Your Supply Chain: Responsible, ethical, and transparent sourcing at the core
  • Support Sustainable Innovation: Lighter, smarter, and greener component solutions
  • Enhance Brand Value: Deliver the sustainable practices today's customers demand
