Precision Machined Parts – Aluminum & Steel

About Us

At Dessimate, we specialize in providing precision-machined parts manufactured from Aluminum and Steel at our trusted facilities in India.

We deliver world-class quality, competitive pricing, and on-time shipments to clients across the United States, supporting industries that demand the highest standards for mechanical performance and dimensional accuracy.

From simple components to complex, tight-tolerance parts, we offer a complete, reliable supply chain solution tailored to your needs.

Our Capabilities

⚙️ CNC Machining

  • 3-axis, 4-axis, and 5-axis CNC milling and turning
  • High-volume production and small-batch prototyping
  • Precision tolerances up to ±0.001 inches
  • CAD/CAM programming support

🧰 Secondary Operations

  • Tapping, drilling, threading, boring
  • Heat treatment and hardening
  • Surface finishes: anodizing, powder coating, plating
  • Assembly and sub-assembly services

🔩 Materials We Machine

  • Aluminum Grades: 6061, 6082, 7075, ADC12, A356
  • Steel Grades: Mild Steel, Stainless Steel (304, 316), Alloy Steel, Carbon Steel
