High-Quality Aluminum and Steel Castings

About Us

At Dessimate, we specialize in supplying precision mechanical parts castings using Aluminum and Steel to customers across the United States.

With a strong network of ISO-certified foundries and decades of expertise, we deliver high-performance die cast and investment cast parts that meet your quality, cost, and delivery expectations.

Whether you need small, intricate components or large structural castings, we provide tailored solutions for automotive, industrial machinery, aerospace, agriculture, energy, and more.

Our Capabilities

🛠 Die Casting (Aluminum and Steel)

  • High-pressure and low-pressure die casting
  • Complex geometry parts with tight tolerances
  • Thin-wall components
  • Post-machining, surface finishing, and assembly services
  • Materials: A356, ADC12, LM6, Mild Steel, Stainless Steel grades

🔥 Investment Casting (Steel and Aluminum Alloys)

  • Lost wax precision casting process
  • Intricate designs and near-net-shape production
  • Aerospace-grade alloys and high-strength steels
  • Heat treatment, shot blasting, and CNC machining are available
